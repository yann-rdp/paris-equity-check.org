Citation

If you use any of the data or images at the Paris-equity-check.org, we would appreciate it if you let us know by sending a link or short description to yann.rdp@climate-energy-college.org. Please cite as:

Paris-equity-check.org – A multidimensional equity and warming assessments of national climate pledges, Robiou du Pont, Y.

Results from the Equity Map should be cited as:

Robiou du Pont, Y. et al. Equitable mitigation to achieve the Paris Agreement goals. Nature Climate Change 7, (2017), available at: http://dx.doi.org/10.1038/nclimate3186.

Results from the Pledged Warming Map should be cited as:

Robiou du Pont, Y. et al. Warming assessment of the bottom-up Paris Agreement emissions pledges. Nature Communications 7, (2018), available at: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-018-07223-9.
