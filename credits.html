Paris-Equity-Check.org – How fair are countries' climate pledges?

This website presents an assessment of countries’ climate pledges under five visions of climate justice and for emissions pathways that consistent with the Paris Agreement goals of limiting warming to 1.5 °C or well below 2 °C.

The Equity Map shows how many of the five visions of climate justice are met by each country’s 2030 pledge. A country will have a number of stars corresponding to the number of visions of climate justice met by their Nationally Determined Contributions.

Clicking on a country will show a country graph with the corresponding equitable national emissions pathways as well as the country’s declaration regarding the fairness of its pledge.

The methods behind the modelling of the equitable emissions allocations can be found in the “The Science”.
